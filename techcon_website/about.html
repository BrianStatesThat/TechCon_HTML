Our History

TechCon began in 2010 as a small gathering of 200 technology enthusiasts in San Francisco. Founded by industry veterans Mark Johnson and Sarah Chen, the conference was created to bridge the gap between academic research and industry practice.

Over the past 14 years, TechCon has grown into one of the world's premier technology conferences, hosting over 15,000 attendees annually across three continents. Our journey reflects the explosive growth of the tech industry itself.

Our Mission

TechCon exists to accelerate technological progress through knowledge sharing and collaboration. We believe that by bringing together the brightest minds across disciplines, we can solve the world's most pressing challenges.

Core Principles:

  • Foster innovation through cross-disciplinary exchange
  • Democratize access to cutting-edge knowledge
  • Build meaningful connections across the tech ecosystem
  • Inspire the next generation of technologists
